# Payment Failure Troubleshooting

A payment may fail for several reasons.

## Common Causes

- Insufficient account balance
- Expired credit card
- Incorrect payment information
- Bank security restrictions
- Temporary payment gateway issues

## Resolution Steps

1. Verify card details.
2. Confirm available funds.
3. Contact your financial institution.
4. Retry the transaction.
5. Use an alternative payment method if necessary.

## Automatic Retries

The system automatically retries failed payments within a defined retry schedule.

## Service Impact

Continued payment failures may result in:

- Subscription suspension
- Restricted platform access
- Account notifications

## Recommended Action

Update your payment method promptly to prevent service interruptions.

If multiple attempts continue to fail, contact support with the transaction timestamp and invoice number.
