# Browser Cookie Clearing Guide

Cookies store temporary website information and may occasionally cause login or session issues.

## Google Chrome

1. Open Settings.
2. Select Privacy and Security.
3. Click Clear Browsing Data.
4. Select Cookies and Cached Images.
5. Click Clear Data.

## Microsoft Edge

1. Open Settings.
2. Navigate to Privacy, Search, and Services.
3. Select Clear Browsing Data.
4. Remove Cookies and Cached Files.

## Mozilla Firefox

1. Open Settings.
2. Navigate to Privacy and Security.
3. Select Clear Data.
4. Choose Cookies and Site Data.

## After Clearing

- Restart your browser.
- Log in again.
- Verify issue resolution.

Cookie clearing often resolves authentication, session timeout, and page-loading problems.
