# Account Lockout Recovery

Accounts may become temporarily locked for security reasons.

## Common Causes

- Multiple failed login attempts
- Suspicious login activity
- Security policy violations

## Recovery Steps

1. Wait for the lockout period to expire.
2. Reset your password.
3. Attempt login again.
4. Enable multi-factor authentication.

## Security Recommendations

To prevent future lockouts:

- Use strong passwords.
- Avoid password sharing.
- Enable MFA whenever available.

## Administrative Unlock

Organization administrators may unlock managed accounts through the administration console.

## Support Assistance

If the account remains locked after the waiting period, contact support and provide your registered email address.
