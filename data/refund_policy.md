# Refund Policy

Customer satisfaction is important to us. Refund requests are reviewed according to the following guidelines.

## Eligibility

Refunds may be considered when:

- A duplicate charge occurred.
- A technical issue prevented service access.
- A billing error was identified.

## Non-Refundable Cases

Refunds are generally not provided for:

- Partial subscription usage
- Feature dissatisfaction after active usage
- Missed cancellation deadlines

## Request Process

1. Submit a refund request through support.
2. Provide invoice information.
3. Describe the issue in detail.
4. Await review from the billing team.

## Processing Time

Refund reviews typically take 3-5 business days.

Approved refunds are processed to the original payment method.

## Escalation

Complex billing disputes may require manual review by a billing specialist.

Customers will receive status updates throughout the review process.
