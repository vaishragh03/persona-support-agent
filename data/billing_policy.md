# Billing Policy

All subscriptions are billed automatically according to the selected billing cycle.

## Billing Cycles

- Monthly
- Quarterly
- Annual

Charges are processed automatically on the renewal date.

## Invoice Availability

Invoices are generated after successful payment and can be downloaded from the Billing Dashboard.

## Failed Payments

If a payment fails:

1. The system retries payment automatically.
2. Notifications are sent to account administrators.
3. Service access may be restricted after multiple failed attempts.

## Billing Adjustments

Billing adjustments may occur in the following situations:

- Subscription upgrades
- Subscription downgrades
- Prorated service changes

## Important Notes

- Taxes may vary by region.
- Charges appear under the company billing descriptor.
- Customers are responsible for maintaining valid payment methods.

For billing disputes or account-specific questions, contact customer support.
