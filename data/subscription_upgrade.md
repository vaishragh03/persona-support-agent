# Subscription Upgrade Guide

Customers may upgrade their subscription plan at any time.

## Upgrade Process

1. Navigate to Billing Settings.
2. Select Change Plan.
3. Choose a higher-tier subscription.
4. Confirm payment details.
5. Complete the upgrade.

## Prorated Billing

Unused time from the current plan is automatically applied toward the upgraded subscription.

## Feature Availability

New features become available immediately after upgrade completion.

## Verification

After upgrading:

- Refresh the application.
- Verify access to premium features.
- Review updated billing information.

## Support

If upgraded features do not appear, contact support with your account ID and invoice details.
